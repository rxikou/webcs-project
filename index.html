At François Gregroire, we believe that luxury should be accessible to everyone. As an online boutique, we specialize in bringing niche fragrances from iconic perfume houses — including Jean Paul Gaultier, Maison Margiela, and Maison Francis Kurkdjian — to the Philippine market.
